<h6>Get To Know The Lingo</h6>
<p>Admittedly this is my first foray into establishing an online business so I consider myself to be an <a href="https://twitter.com/PyRunner" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://twitter.com/PyRunner', 'infant blogger');" target="_blank">infant blogger</a>, but I do learn very fast when it comes to technology. <li>Click Through Rate (CTR) &#8211; the percentage of site visitors that interact with an ad on your website (total ad clicks divided by impressions)</li>

<li>Impressions &#8211; when users get served ads (regardless of clicks our bounces away)</li>
<li>Cost Per Thousand (CPM) &#8211; the amount you get paid (regardless of clicks) per 1000 visitors on your website</li>

<h6>Engineering A Target Number</h6>
<p>A business works off of operating costs plus profit margins and I am no different than any other business. Let&#8217;s first create a target number using a break-even analysis:</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Break Even = cost of server + cost of domain * (months I&#8217;m in business theoretically)</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">$100 = ($10 + $15) * (4 months)</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Now we know that our break even point is $100 over 4 months, let&#8217;s take a look at where we stand. Using Google Analytics linked with Google AdSense, we can develop a monetization strategy for the next 4 months.</p>
